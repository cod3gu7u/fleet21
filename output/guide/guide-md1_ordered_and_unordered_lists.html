Ordered ¶

Tight:

  1. First
  2. Second
  3. Third

and:

  1. One
  2. Two
  3. Three

Loose using tabs:

  1. First

  2. Second

  3. Third

and using spaces:

  1. One

  2. Two

  3. Three

Multiple paragraphs:

  1. Item 1, graf one.

    Item 2. graf two. The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog's back.

  2. Item 2.

  3. Item 3.
